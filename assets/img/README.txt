MELIORA — station artwork drop-in
==================================

Put PNGs here with these exact names and they appear automatically in the
Map towers (no code changes needed). If a file is missing, the built-in
placeholder icon shows instead.

  brief.png        -> "The Brief" tower
  path.png         -> "The Path" tower
  field.png        -> "The Field" tower
  milestones.png   -> "Milestones" tower

Tips
----
- Transparent background. Roughly square. ~400-600px is plenty.
- Bold art with outlines reads best against the thick black borders.
- Prefer PNG (the site looks for .png). Have SVG instead? Tell me and
  I'll switch the <img> source — SVG scales even better.

RIGHTS — important
------------------
Only use art you OWN or that is genuinely free to host:
  - Storyset (storyset.com)      — free flat illustrations, set your own colour
  - unDraw (undraw.co)           — free, no attribution, recolourable
  - Open Doodles (opendoodles.com) — CC0, hand-drawn
Do NOT drop in screenshots of Canva stock, irasutoya, or signed fan art —
those are licensed/copyrighted and can't be self-hosted here.
